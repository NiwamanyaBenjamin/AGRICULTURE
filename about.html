Green Tree Aid Dealers is a Kampala-based organic food business dedicated to delivering natural produce at affordable prices. Our vision is to create a healthy, eco-friendly future for Ugandans by promoting organic agriculture.

About Us

Green Tree Aid Dealers is a leading provider of high-quality tree care products, tools, and services dedicated to promoting sustainable forestry management and environmental conservation across Uganda and beyond.

Founded with a passion for green living and ecosystem preservation, our company supplies essential products such as eco-friendly tree fertilizers, healthy seedlings, modern pruning tools, organic treatment solutions, and sustainable planting materials.

At Green Tree Aid Dealers, we work closely with farmers, landscapers, schools, and community projects to promote urban greening and reforestation. Our mission is to empower individuals and institutions with the knowledge and tools they need to grow and maintain healthy trees while protecting our planet.

With years of experience in the tree care and nursery industry, our team is committed to offering customer-centered service, expert advice, and products that deliver real results. Whether you are planting a single tree or restoring an entire forest, Green Tree Aid Dealers is your trusted partner in environmental sustainability.

Together, let's plant the seeds of a greener tomorrow.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be at the forefront of environmental conservation by providing high-quality, sustainable products and services to individuals and organizations committed to preserving nature. We aim to improve urban landscapes and empower communities to take charge of their environmental future.

Our Vision

Our vision is a world where every community has access to tools, knowledge, and resources to maintain healthy trees and green spaces, contributing to a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable environment for future generations.
